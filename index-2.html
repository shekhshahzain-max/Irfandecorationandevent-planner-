About Us

Karachi's trusted paper & event decor specialists

For every occasion — walima, mehndi, aqeeqah, birthdays or a corporate launch — we design and build the decor by hand: paper backdrops, balloon walls, floral stages and themed setups tailored to your budget and vision.
